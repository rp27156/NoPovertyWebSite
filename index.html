Say No To Poverty

Our Goal is to remove poverty

The burden poverty places on society and individuals isn’t just economic or physical. Measuring poverty this way overlooks the other types of poverty oppressing the marginalized. Poverty causes the poor to suffer emotionally and spiritually as well. That’s why the SDG isn’t just about ending poverty around the world. It’s about protecting the planet and ensuring all people enjoy peace and prosperity. Building a sustainable future means finding ways to meet the needs of the present without sacrificing the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It requires a unified, collaborative and global approach that isn’t bound by cultures, conditions or continents. Because a life of poverty means the poor carry a shade of poverty in their hearts and wear it etched on their faces.
